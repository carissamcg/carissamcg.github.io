Cabinetry

At Ptarmigan, we make more than just kitchens and bathrooms. From walk-in closets to offices, we can provide you with beautiful, functional cabinet solutions for any space whether that is in your home or business. Solid wood is timeless in style, so you know your investment will grow with you and your home over the years.
